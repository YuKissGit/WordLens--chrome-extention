Note:
• This extension does not collect or upload your word data. All data stays on your local browser.
• The word list does not sync across devices.
• If you switch your Chrome account or use another browser, your saved words will not be available.
• If you clear browser data, uninstall the extension, or reinstall the browser, your saved words may be permanently deleted.
• To ensure stable performance, the word book can store up to 100 words. When the limit is reached, please clear wordbook before adding new ones.
